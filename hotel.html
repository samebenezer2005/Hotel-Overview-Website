Rooftop Restaurants

Several rooftop restaurants in Chennai offer dining with scenic views. Swiggy lists popular options like KHAAS - The Elevated Indian Kitchen & Bar, Hard Rock Cafe, Red Sky Cafe, and Roger That. These restaurants offer a variety of cuisines, including North Indian, Indian, American, Chinese, and South Indian, with prices ranging from ₹1000 to ₹2200 for two. Some offer discounts and deals, and allow table bookings.

Family Dining Restaurants

Family-style restaurants offer a relaxed dining experience with larger portions served on platters for everyone to share. Prices vary depending on the cuisine, location, and specific restaurant, but generally, they are considered casual dining establishments with a focus on affordability and a welcoming atmosphere for families and groups.

Pre Bookings
PJS is a hotel search platform that allows users to compare prices from various booking sites. It does not handle bookings directly, but rather directs users to partner websites to complete their reservations. To pre-book a hotel using Trivago, you would first search for your desired destination and dates on their platform, then select a hotel and be redirected to a booking site to finalize the reservation and potentially make a prepayment.
Online Bookings Available
PJS is a metasearch engine that allows users to compare hotel prices from various online booking sites. It doesn't handle bookings directly; instead, it redirects users to the booking site where they can finalize their reservation and payment.
